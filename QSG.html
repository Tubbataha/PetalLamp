Quick Start Guide to
install the Petal Lamp

The Lamp is packaged in a card tube, with rigid end caps secured by clips and screws.  


 1.   Place the package on a table


  1.    Remove 3 clips from bottom of tube by pulling straight out from base.


3. Remove the top cover and hold base with one hand while lifting tube up sliding over the lamp, leaving the lamp secured to the base by 3 screws under the base.




  4.  Remove lamp from the base : 

pick the lamp up and turn on side holding the lamp in one hand while looking under the base to unscrew three plastic fasteners with your fingers, put the lamp down, up-right on the table.

       `

 
  1.     Inspect the lamp
    - if any wooden petals are loose carefully re-attach to the petal spines- lower hook first into round hole first and hold with a finger then stretch the petal gently to hook over the upper hook.

  1.  Connecting power
  Locate a suitable socket - standard outlet for which ever country -  the exact power supply appearance may vary.  The petal lamp is best located away from direct sunlight and strong drafts,
and needs enough space for the petals to fully open.

    plug in power cord into wall first, then to socket on base of the lamp.

  1.    The Reset action : When first powered the lamp needs to reset itself to closed, the petal LEDS may flash several times before movement stops.

  1.     Confirm the lamp is OK- touch the leaf button.  Only a soft touch is needed 

    Touch leaf button - opens- slowly with center colour a default hue
    Touch again closes - slowly, lights dimming as it closes


 ->  To get full control of all the features the lamp needs to connected to a Wifi network so it can be controlled by your smartphone or PC


  1.  Setup Wifi connection
When the lamp does not find a WiFi network it recognises, it creates its own temporary Wifi network. You can to connect to that special network called 'Flower-WIFI',using a phone or PC and configure the petal lamp to join your local Wifi and then your phone and the petal lamp will reconnect to your local Wifi.
To connect the lamp to your local Wifi and control it you need to know something about your Wifi , you can find this information with your phone. It is a good idea to write these down.

A. Find your Wifi network name (SSID) and password
B. An available IP address for the lamp, on your phone open the Wifi option and select the network you are connected to, look for advanced options and then find the ip address : for example it may look like 10 . 0 . 4 . 50 It is always a sequence of 4 number parts separated by dots
C. A good fixed address for the lamp is to use first 3 parts of your phone ip address and '200' or higher as 4th part : so in the example if your phone address is 192.168.1.45 use 192.168.1.200

The red and blue light will show and the lamp has created its own wifi network 'Flower-WIFI" for configuration

Control by phone


You can now control the lamp with this page,
Controls- Open and Close operate the lamp.
State: confirms when the lamp is Open, Closing, Closed, or Opening.
Max Brightness control slides to adjust the maximum brightness of the white petal LEDS
the Red, Green and Blue sliders adjust the colour of the lamp center.
The Touch sensitivity adjusts the response of the leaf button. It makes the button more or less sensitive to a finger touch if needed.


Create shortcut on phone

Having a shortcut or bookmark icon on your phone allows you to access the control page without remembering the address.
On an Android device - With the Petal lamp control page showing, press the three dots top right on screen, and choose 'Add to Home screen'
You can then change the name for the shortcut and confirm to add the icon to the home screen of your phone.


On an iPhone use Add bookmark button, lower right on screen, to create a bookmark in favourites
